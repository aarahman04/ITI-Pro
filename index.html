Who We Are

A Complete EV Ecosystem

ITI Pro bridges the gap between electric vehicle technology and skilled manpower. Founded in Hyderabad, we have grown into a national organisation spanning training centres, service hubs, sales networks, and franchise partners across 12+ states.

Our programs are designed in partnership with OEMs, government bodies, and industry experts — ensuring every certification we issue carries genuine market value.
