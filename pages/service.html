FAQ

Service Questions

Which vehicle types do you service?+
We service all electric two-wheelers (e-scooters, e-bikes, e-mopeds) and electric three-wheelers (e-rickshaws, cargo EVs, passenger e-autos) from all major brands including Hero Electric, Ola, Ather, Ampere, Okinawa, Mahindra Electric, and more. Contact us if you're unsure whether your vehicle model is covered.
Do you use OEM parts or aftermarket parts?+
We always recommend and prefer OEM-authorised or OEM-equivalent parts. If you request budget aftermarket parts, we will inform you clearly and note it in your service report. All parts are billed at MRP with an original purchase invoice provided to you.
What is covered under the 30-day workmanship warranty?+
The 30-day workmanship warranty covers the labour and repair work performed by our technician. If the same fault recurs within 30 days of a repair job due to technician error or improper installation, we redo the work at no charge. The warranty does not cover new faults, physical damage, or issues arising from misuse after service.
How do I book an AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract)?+
AMC can be booked via our contact form, by phone, or at any ITI Pro service centre. The AMC covers 3 scheduled service visits per year, priority booking, and a 10% discount on all parts. Fleet AMC (for 5+ vehicles) includes additional visits and a dedicated account manager. Pricing varies by vehicle type and location.
