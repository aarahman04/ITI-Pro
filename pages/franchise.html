Legal Framework

The Franchise Agreement

A fair, transparent agreement designed to protect both parties and create a long-term, mutually beneficial partnership.

Scope
What the Agreement Covers

Brand usage rights, territorial exclusivity boundaries, training curriculum license, service SOP license, equipment sourcing rights, trainer certification entitlements, marketing support obligations, revenue sharing structure, quality audit schedule, and renewal and exit clauses. Duration is 3 years (5 years for Master Franchise), fully renewable.

Territory
Exclusive Territory Rights

Each franchise is granted a clearly defined exclusive territory — a city zone, district, or state depending on the model — within which ITI Pro will not open, license, or authorise another centre during the active agreement period. Territory boundaries are defined in the agreement schedule and cannot be unilaterally altered by either party.

Ongoing Support
What ITI Pro Provides Continuously

Bi-annual curriculum updates, trainer refresher certifications, national marketing campaigns benefiting all centres, student placement support through our central cell, quarterly quality audits with detailed feedback reports, access to the partner portal, and a dedicated franchise relationship manager for the duration of the agreement.

Quality Standards
Maintaining the ITI Pro Standard

Franchise partners agree to deliver training and service to ITI Pro's defined quality benchmarks, use only approved branding and curriculum, maintain certified trainer ratios, and participate in quarterly audits. Non-compliance follows a structured improvement notice process before any escalation — we work with partners, not against them.

Fees & Financials
Revenue Sharing & Fees

A one-time franchise fee is payable upon agreement signing. A monthly revenue share (8–15% depending on model) is reported and remitted by the 10th of the following month. No hidden royalties or surprise charges. Annual audit of financials is required. Approved government grant income (if applicable) is excluded from revenue share calculation.

Exit & Renewal
Exit and Renewal Terms

Franchisees may exit with a 90-day written notice after the first agreement year. No penalty applies if the centre is in good standing. Agreements are renewable every 3 years subject to performance review — high-performing partners receive a discounted renewal fee. In the event of a mutually agreed exit, ITI Pro will help transition students and service customers to the nearest alternative centre.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience in the EV industry to open a franchise?+
No prior EV industry experience is required for the Training Studio or Training + Service Hub models. ITI Pro's Train-the-Trainer program and trainer certification process equips your team with the required technical knowledge. We do recommend a background in business management or education management for the Master Franchise model.
Can I open a franchise in a tier-2 or tier-3 city?+
Absolutely. Tier-2 and tier-3 cities are among our highest-priority expansion zones. EV adoption, ITI graduate density, and lower competition often create stronger unit economics in smaller cities than in metros. Several of our best-performing franchise centres are in tier-2 cities like Warangal, Davangere, Hubli, Rajahmundry, and Nanded.
Can I get government grants or PMEGP funding for the franchise?+
Yes, many franchise partners have used PMEGP, MUDRA, and State Skill Mission subsidies to fund part of their setup costs. ITI Pro's partnership with NSDC and State Skill Missions also makes franchise centres eligible for per-trainee government grants under specific skill development schemes. Our franchise team will guide you on applicable schemes in your state.
What happens if I want to expand after opening one centre?+
Existing franchise partners are given first right of refusal on adjacent territories before we open them to new applicants. High-performing single-centre partners can also apply to upgrade to a Master Franchise arrangement, giving them the right to open and sub-franchise multiple centres in their region.
How soon can I open after signing the agreement?+
Most Training Studio partners open within 45–60 days of signing. Training + Service Hub partners typically open in 60–90 days, depending on fit-out requirements. Master Franchise territory development is a rolling process with no fixed opening date. Our onboarding team provides a week-by-week launch checklist to keep you on track.
