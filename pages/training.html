FAQ

Common Questions

Who can enrol in ITI Pro training programs?+
Anyone with a minimum 10th/12th pass qualification can enrol in basic-level programs. ITI graduates, diploma holders, and B.Tech / B.E. students can enrol in advanced certifications. We also offer dedicated tracks for experienced auto mechanics and ICE technicians transitioning to EV.
Are ITI Pro certificates recognised by the government?+
Yes. Our programs are aligned with the National Skills Qualifications Framework (NSQF) and are affiliated with NSDC. Several programs carry AICTE, MSME, and State Skill Mission certifications. Certificates are accepted by all our 200+ hiring partners as proof of qualification.
What is the fee structure and are there scholarships?+
Programs range from free/low-cost seminars (₹0–999) to 6-month certification courses (₹8,000–60,000). EMI options (0% interest, 3–6 months) are available for all programmes above ₹10,000. SC/ST/OBC and girl-student scholarships are available through our government tie-ups. Contact our admissions team for current scholarship availability in your state.
Is placement guaranteed after completing a course?+
We maintain a 98% placement rate for graduates of our 3-month and above certificate programs. While individual outcomes depend on performance, every graduate receives placement support — including resume review, mock interviews, referrals to hiring partners, and access to our bi-monthly campus recruitment drives until placed.
Can institutions affiliate with ITI Pro to offer these programs?+
Yes. Schools, polytechnics, ITI colleges, and engineering institutions can partner with ITI Pro to offer our certified curriculum on their campus. We provide the content, trainer certification, equipment checklist, and branding. Contact our franchise/partnership team for affiliation pricing and timelines.
Are programs available online?+
Seminar and theory modules of certification courses are available online via our learning portal. However, hands-on lab sessions, workshop programs, and industrial visits require physical attendance at an ITI Pro centre or affiliated venue. Hybrid models (online theory + local lab access) are available in cities with partner institutions.
