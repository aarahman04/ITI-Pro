FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly will I get a response?+
Most enquiries are responded to within 24 hours on business days. Service booking requests are typically confirmed within 2 hours during office hours. Franchise enquiries receive a callback within 2 working days from the franchise team.
Do you have offices outside Hyderabad?+
Yes. While our head office is in Hyderabad, we have 50+ franchise and company-owned centres across 12+ states including Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Delhi NCR. Contact us to find your nearest centre.
Can I visit the head office without an appointment?+
We recommend booking an appointment to ensure the right team member is available for you. Walk-ins are welcome during office hours for general enquiries, but specialist meetings (franchise, partnerships, media) require prior scheduling.
Which languages does your support team speak?+
Our central support team works in English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, and Kannada. Centre-level support in other regional languages is available at the respective franchise locations.
