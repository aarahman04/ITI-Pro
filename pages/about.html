Our Journey

Built on Purpose

ITI Pro was founded with a single conviction: India's EV revolution needed not just vehicles, but a skilled workforce to maintain, repair, and drive adoption of those vehicles across every city and town.

Starting from a single training centre in Hyderabad with a team of five engineers, we grew into a national organisation with franchise hubs, service networks, and a thriving community of over 5,000 alumni.

Our programs are co-designed with leading OEMs, government skill bodies, and industry leaders — ensuring the certificates we award unlock real jobs, real businesses, and real futures.

2013
Founded in Hyderabad

ITI Pro began as a 5-person team running EV awareness workshops and basic repair training for local ITI graduates.

2015
First Certification Program

Launched our flagship EV Technician Certification — the first of its kind in Telangana, with 200 enrolments in Year 1.

2017
Franchise Model Launched

Expanded to Bengaluru and Pune with our franchise model, empowering local partners to run ITI Pro-branded centres.

2019
NSDC Government Partnership

Signed MoUs with NSDC and State Skill Missions for officially recognised EV certifications across 4 states.

2022
5,000+ Trained Milestone

Crossed 5,000 certified EV professionals. Expanded to 12 states with 50+ active franchise and company-owned centres.

2024
National EV Expo Launch

Launched ITI Pro EV Expo — India's fastest-growing EV industry event, drawing 200+ brands and 10,000+ visitors.
